Portkvarteren

Approx. 7000 m² Brunnshög Lund

Lund.

Sveaviken Bostad / Moko / Arkitekterna Krook & Tjäder.

A housing block consisting of three simple volumes in wood and slate. One of the volumes features a large rooftop greenhouse, integrated in the overall design where cucumbers are produced and sold to the local supermarket as an experimental local food production project. Moreover, the block hosts a number of public and communal areas, like a café, co-working space and a workshop.
